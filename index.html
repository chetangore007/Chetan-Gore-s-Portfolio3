About Chetan!

I am a First-Year Computer Engineering student at MIT Academy of Engineering, Alandi Devachi, Pune. Passionate about technology, innovation, and creative problem-solving, I constantly strive to excel in everything I do. Whether it's academics, projects, or extracurriculars, I take on every challenge with enthusiasm and determination. I have a strong foundation in programming, logical thinking, and analytical skills. I enjoy working on diverse technical projects—whether it's building applications, exploring AI, or visualizing data—and I’m always curious to learn more and push my boundaries.

What sets me apart is my ability to adapt quickly, think critically, and work effectively both independently and in teams. I believe in continuous improvement, and I’m committed to growing into a well-rounded engineer who not only solves problems but also makes a meaningful impact.
